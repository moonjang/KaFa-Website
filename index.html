Visual Identity Design for
Korean American Faculty Association at UGA

KAFA is the association for Korean and Korean-American faculty at UGA. The increasing hate crime against Asians amidst COVID-19 pandemic (including the tragic shootings in Atlanta last March, which claimed the lives of eight innocent people including six women of Asian descent) has motivated Korean American faculty to organize and create this association. The purpose of KAFA at UGA includes: (a) continuing to raise awareness of Asian American issues through AAPI speaker series, (b) Increasing the visibility of Korean American faculty members and scholars, and (c) supporting Korean American students through mentoring and scholarships.
